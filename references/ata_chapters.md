## Check Types by ATA Focus

### A-Check (Light Check ~500 FH)
Primary ATA: 05, 12, 27, 28, 29, 32, 33, 36

### C-Check (Heavy Check ~6000 FH)
All ATA chapters including structures (51-57)

### Engine Shop Visit
ATA 70-80, plus related airframe ATA 36, 73, 79

### Structural Inspection
ATA 51-57 (SRM rather than AMM for repairs)
